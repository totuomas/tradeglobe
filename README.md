## Trade Globe

[Website](https://totuomas.github.io/tradeglobe/) to visualize the international trade and countries main trade partners

## ✨ Features

* 🌐 **Interactive globe**
  * Click any country to explore its export partners

* 📊 **Trade data visualization**

  * Countries are colored based on their share of exports
  * Even small trade relationships are visible with enhanced scaling

* 📋 **Side panel insights**

  * Displays top export partners with percentages
  * Updates dynamically on click

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **HTML5**
* **CSS3**
* **JavaScript (ES6+)**
* **Globe.gl**
* **Node.js (Express)**
* **UN Comtrade API**
